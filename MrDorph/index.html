Welcome

This website is for EAB members to keep up with scheduled events, post comments to other clan members and generally gain as much information as you can.

EAB is English speaking and over 16s only!

The English armoured brigade is a clan, of which members are associated with world of tanks. We are a community of players who share a common love of PC games and tanks. We strive to gain success in matches and to make our name noticed among The World of Tanks community.
